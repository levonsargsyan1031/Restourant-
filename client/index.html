About Us

Time O'clock, is a compilation of lively dining experiences. Time O'clock celebrates the best of Armenian, Italian American dishes with welcoming hospitality and service. Our menu is a walk around the country — and the world. Restaurant also offers a “For the Table” chef-curated option for a family-style experience, offered evening alongside the full menu. The Time O'clock restaurant, a place to sit and read, eat and watch the world. Welcome to our restaurant and get to know our small, passionate, and hardworking crew of folks
